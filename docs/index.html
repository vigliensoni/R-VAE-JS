This is R-VAE-JS

a rhythmic model player and visualizer for R-VAE

Press Go! to load a model and play

GO!

Instructions:

1. Move the mouse in the performance space

2. Click on the space to retrieve and decode a rhythm

3. Drag the mouse to interpolate rhythms

4. Change the Threshold to modify the number of onsets retrieved

5. Change the Noise to add variability to the position of the mouse

6. Mute individuals instruments by pressing Q, W, or E

7. Mute all by pressing R
